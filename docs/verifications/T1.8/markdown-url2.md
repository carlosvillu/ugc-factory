Why You'll Love It


Meet Analog: The Simplest Productivity System. Analog is a physical companion for your digital tools that helps you prioritize and focus on your most important tasks.

The Analog Starter Kit includes:

- **1 Month of Analog Task Cards**


30 Today cards, 2 Next Day cards, 1 Someday card

- **Wood Card Holder**


The wood card holder keeps your Today card in front of you while the open-back is designed to store your completed cards.

- **Card Divider**


The included tabbed divider gives you an easy way to separate your new and completed cards

## I’m more so the fisherman.

In a moment, I will tell you everything you need to know about Ugmonk.

But, first, a story...

Once upon a time, a fisherman taught a banker a lesson that would change his life.

The banker was vacationing in a small coastal village when a fisherman docked beside him with a beautiful catch of tuna. The banker complimented him and asked how long it took.

"Just a little while," replied the fisherman.

The banker asked why he didn't stay out longer and catch more.

"Because I have enough to support my family," the fisherman replied. "I enjoy sleeping late, playing with my children, taking afternoon naps with my wife, and strolling into the village in the evenings to sip wine and play guitar with my friends."

The banker scoffed. "Spend more time fishing. Buy a bigger boat. Then several boats. Eventually you'll have a fleet, move to a big city, build a real business."

"But then what?" asked the fisherman.

"Then you announce an IPO, sell your stock, become a millionaire."

"But then what?" asked the fisherman, again.

"Then you retire. Move to a small coastal village — sleep late, spend time with your family, sip wine with friends in the evenings."

The fisherman smiled, winked, threw the tuna over his shoulder, and carried his catch into town.

## Ugmonk exists to be that wink.

. . . . .

Hi. I'm Jeff.

I'm a designer by trade and an entrepreneur by accident.

I started Ugmonk in 2008 because I couldn't find simple, well-made things. No investors. No exit strategy. Just a conviction that good design should outlast trends, and that the best products are the ones you never feel the need to replace.

Seventeen years later, that conviction remains true. In a world drowning in notifications and digital noise, the most intentional thing you can do is slow down, pick up a pen, and think on paper.

That's what [Analog](https://ugmonk.com/collections/analog "The Analog System") is for.

A simple card-based system for capturing what matters, planning your day, and staying present. No app. No battery. No algorithm deciding what you see next. Just you, a pen, and a card.

We're a small 4-person team in Downingtown, Pennsylvania. We take months (sometimes years) getting each product right. We obsess over materials, tolerances, and the feeling of a thing in your hand. We ship everything ourselves and reply directly to every email.

Shopping from Ugmonk is a lot like buying from your neighborhood corner store. You're supporting our family and the craftspeople who share our obsession with getting every detail right.

Thanks for coming along on the journey with us!

– Jeff Sheldon
