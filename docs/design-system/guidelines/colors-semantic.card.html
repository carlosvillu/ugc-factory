Not theme-invariant. A hue tuned to glow on a near-black surface collapses on a light one: reused as-is, these five scored 1.99–3.30:1 in light against WCAG AA's 4.5:1 for text. Each light variant is the lightest tone of the same hue that clears the bar — measured on the pair above (the token as text on its own -soft tint), which is what a badge actually renders. Calibrated on the WORST surface, not the best: the ratios shown are on --surface-3; on --surface they reach 5.3. A first pass tuned only against white passed there and failed at 3.9–4.3 where badges really sit (--surface-2, --bg-subtle) — isolating a token from its surface is the same mistake that caused the bug.
